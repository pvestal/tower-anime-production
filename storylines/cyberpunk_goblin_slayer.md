# CYBERPUNK GOBLIN SLAYER: NEON SHADOWS
## Season 1 Story Bible

---

## CORE CONCEPT
A gritty reboot of the 1983 Goblin Slayer, set in 2087 where goblins have evolved alongside technology. They operate as organized crime syndicates, using cyber-enhancement and dark web networks for human trafficking and worse.

---

## MAIN CHARACTER

### KAI NAKAMURA
- **Age**: 24
- **Appearance**: Dark hair with neon blue streaks, glowing cybernetic right eye, angular face with mechanical jaw joints, black tactical vest embedded with glowing circuits
- **Personality**: Stoic exterior hiding deep trauma, struggles between cold efficiency and human empathy
- **Background**: Lost family to goblin trafficking ring at 14, underwent illegal military cyber-enhancement program
- **Internal Conflict**: "Does hunting monsters make me one?"
- **Quirks**: Listens to 90s music during operations, uses cannabis for PTSD, talks to her dead sister's AI ghost

---

## EPISODE STRUCTURE

### EPISODE 1: "NEON DEN"
**Location**: New York Underground
**Opening Scene**:
- First-person POV through Kai's cybernetic eye
- HUD displays: "Day 3,287 since the incident"
- Internal monologue: *"They say you either die a hero or live long enough to become the villain. I'm still deciding which one I am."*

**Act 1 - The Rescue (15 min)**
- Discovery of goblin den beneath abandoned Times Square station
- Victim profile matches missing persons database: Sarah Chen, 35, mother of two
- Tactical approach using cyber-stealth, disabling security feeds
- *Cinematography*: Long take corridor fight (John Wick style), neon-lit shadows

**Act 2 - The Extraction (20 min)**
- Find victim in breeding chamber (implied horror, not shown explicitly)
- Goblin boss: Cyber-enhanced with neural implants, connected to larger network
- Combat: Bullet-time sequences mixed with brutal hand-to-hand
- Sarah's trauma response: "They said no one would come..."

**Act 3 - The Revelation (10 min)**
- Data extraction from goblin boss reveals network spanning continents
- Sarah provides intel: "They mentioned Frankfurt... said the auction was in three days"
- Kai's decision point: Report to authorities (who might be compromised) or go rogue
- Episode ends with Kai booking flight to Frankfurt, internal monologue: *"The hunt continues."*

### EPISODE 2: "BINARY PREDATORS"
**Location**: Frankfurt Financial District
**Theme**: Corporate corruption enabling trafficking

**Key Scenes**:
- Infiltration of tech corporation laundering goblin money
- Introduction of MARCUS STEEL - corporate whistleblower with insider knowledge
- Discovery: Goblins using cryptocurrency and darknet for global operations
- Action: Office building fight, using environment (printers, glass walls, server rooms)
- Revelation: CEO is human-goblin hybrid, new evolution of threat

---

### EPISODE 3: "GHOST IN THE MACHINE"
**Location**: Frankfurt Underground
**Theme**: Technology vs humanity

**Key Scenes**:
- Kai's cybernetic eye gets hacked, forcing her to confront digital vulnerabilities
- Flashback: Young Kai watching her family taken, establishing her "never again" mentality
- Introduction of ELENA - Elite hacker who helps patch Kai's systems
- Major combat: Abandoned factory, strobe lights creating disorienting effect
- Character moment: Kai removes eye temporarily, fighting "blind" using other senses

---

### EPISODE 4: "DESERT SHADOWS"
**Location**: Marrakech Medina
**Theme**: Traditional vs modern hunting methods

**Key Scenes**:
- Tracking goblin slave routes through ancient markets
- Meeting HASSAN - Traditional monster hunter using old techniques
- Culture clash: Kai's tech approach vs Hassan's spiritual methods
- Discovery: Goblins exploiting refugee routes for trafficking
- Cinematic fight: Rooftop chase through old city, parkour elements

---

### EPISODE 5: "SANDSTORM RISING"
**Location**: Sahara Border
**Theme**: Isolation and self-reliance

**Key Scenes**:
- Following trafficking convoy into desert
- Kai's drug use increasing due to stress, affecting performance
- Hallucination sequence: Conversations with dead sister's AI
- Ambush: Goblins using sandstorm as cover for attack
- Moral choice: Save one busload of victims or pursue goblin leadership

---

### EPISODE 6: "PERSIAN NIGHTS"
**Location**: Tehran Underground
**Theme**: Revolution and resistance

**Key Scenes**:
- Connecting with Iranian resistance fighting goblin-backed regime
- Introduction of ZARA - Young fighter who reminds Kai of herself
- Discovery: Government using goblins for population control
- Stealth mission: Infiltrating government facility
- Kai begins training Zara, seeing redemption through mentorship

---

### EPISODE 7: "DIGITAL JIHAD"
**Location**: Cyber-Tehran
**Theme**: Information warfare

**Key Scenes**:
- Cyber-attack on goblin network infrastructure
- Kai's eye malfunction revealing hidden memories of enhancement procedure
- Parallel action: Physical and digital battlefield
- Betrayal: Marcus Steel revealed as double agent
- Cliffhanger: Zara captured by goblins

---

### EPISODE 8: "BLOOD PROTOCOLS"
**Location**: Multi-city
**Theme**: Sacrifice and choice

**Key Scenes**:
- Coordinated strikes across all previous locations
- Kai must choose: Save Zara or stop goblin "ascension" ritual
- Revelation: Goblins attempting to merge with AI, becoming digital entities
- Epic fight: Bullet-time mixed with glitch aesthetics
- Cost: Hassan dies protecting refugees

---

### EPISODE 9: "NEURAL BURN"
**Location**: Digital realm/New York
**Theme**: Man vs machine evolution

**Key Scenes**:
- Kai enters cyberspace to fight digital goblins
- Physical body vulnerable while consciousness battles
- Elena and remaining team defend Kai's body
- Inside cyberspace: Tron-meets-Matrix aesthetic
- Kai confronts digitized goblin collective consciousness

---

### EPISODE 10: "NEON REQUIEM"
**Location**: New York - Full circle
**Theme**: Redemption and continuation

**Key Scenes**:
- Final confrontation in original Times Square den
- Goblin King: Fully cyber-enhanced, near-human intelligence
- Kai's choice: Kill him or use him to take down entire network
- Pyrrhic victory: Network destroyed but Kai permanently changed
- Epilogue: New threats emerging, Kai training next generation
- Final shot: Kai's reflection, more machine than human now

## CINEMATOGRAPHY NOTES

### Visual Style:
- **Color Palette**: Neon blues/pinks contrasting with dark grays
- **Camera Work**: Long takes for action, handheld for tension
- **Lighting**: Practical neon sources, harsh shadows
- **VFX**: Minimal CGI, practical effects preferred
- **Aspect Ratio**: 2.35:1 for cinematic feel

### Action Choreography:
- **Inspiration**: John Wick's gun-fu, Oldboy's hallway fight
- **Style**: Brutal efficiency, every move has consequence
- **Slow Motion**: Used sparingly for impact moments
- **First Person**: POV shots through cybernetic eye during key moments

## THEMES & SUBTEXT

### Core Themes:
1. **Human vs Monster**: What defines humanity when you're part machine?
2. **Trauma & Recovery**: Can violence heal violence?
3. **Evolution**: Both species adapting, who wins?
4. **Connection**: Finding family in unexpected places
5. **Identity**: Maintaining self when constantly changing

### Social Commentary:
- Human trafficking in digital age
- Corporate complicity in crime
- Technology as liberation vs oppression
- Refugee exploitation
- Government corruption

## WORLD-BUILDING DETAILS

### Goblin Evolution:
- **Gen 1** (1983-2020): Traditional, primitive
- **Gen 2** (2020-2050): Tool users, organized crime
- **Gen 3** (2050-2070): Cyber-enhanced, networked
- **Gen 4** (2070-present): Human hybrids, AI integration

### Technology:
- **Cybernetic Enhancement**: Common but regulated
- **AI Ghosts**: Digital copies of deceased consciousness
- **Darknet**: Parallel internet for criminal activity
- **Neural Interfaces**: Direct brain-computer connection
- **Bio-hacking**: DIY enhancement culture

## TONE & ATMOSPHERE

### Writing Style:
- **Dialogue**: Minimal, impactful, noir-influenced
- **Internal Monologue**: Stream of consciousness, honest
- **Humor**: Dark, situational, never forced
- **Pacing**: Slow burn punctuated by explosive action

### Mood:
- **Overall**: Oppressive but with hope glimmers
- **Action**: Visceral, consequential
- **Quiet Moments**: Melancholic, introspective
- **Character Interactions**: Guarded becoming genuine
